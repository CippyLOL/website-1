TechLadies is a community-led initiative for women to connect, learn, and grow in the tech industry as software engineers, product managers and designers.

Our community consists of women learning technology to enhance their careers and women already in tech.

How we started

TechLadies was started by Elisha in 2016. As someone who learned programming from friends in the developer community as an adult, Elisha wanted to create a space where women like her can learn technical skills and maybe even switch careers in the tech industry.

TechLadies is led by a group of volunteers in its leadership team.
